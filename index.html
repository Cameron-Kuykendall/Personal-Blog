About

Hi, I’m Cameron — a computer science student, aspiring AI engineer, and lifelong learner on a mission to land a role at OpenAI. This blog is where I document my journey from fundamentals to frontier: from writing clean Python code to experimenting with neural networks and building intelligent tools.

Beyond code, I have a deep passion for the environment, therapy and personal growth, and helping others succeed. I’m especially interested in where technology intersects with these more natural and human aspects of life — how we can use AI not just for efficiency, but for healing, connection, and sustainability.

If you’re exploring similar questions or just learning to build cool things, welcome. Let’s grow together.
